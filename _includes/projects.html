My Projects

My BSc final thesis consisted on applying neural networks to find the fundamental state of a quantum many-body system. Basically, I made use of a library named NetKet (https://www.netket.org) to try to make a simple NN structure, like a RBM or a FFNN, learn the fundamental state of a given many-body system. The plan was to study a couple of different hamiltonians and check how these ML methods applied to physics can be useful and what can we expect from them.
